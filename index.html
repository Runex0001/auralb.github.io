FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is AuraLB safe to use?
Yes. AuraLB operates entirely client-side and injects directly into memory, leaving no traces in the file system. Our "Eject" feature also clears all strings from memory instantly if needed.
Does it work on Realms and Official Servers?
Absolutely. Realms and other featured servers are fully supported. We have specific modules designed to bypass server-side anti-cheat measures like Sentinel and Flare.
Is AuraLB free?
We offer a comprehensive free version with over 80 modules. However, AuraLB Premium unlocks cloud configs, exclusive bypasses, private scripts, and priority support.
How do I install custom scripts?
Navigate to the "Scripts" tab in the ClickGUI, click "Open Folder", and drag your .lua files in. Type `.reload` in chat to refresh. You can also download community scripts directly from our Cloud tab.
What operating systems are supported?
AuraLB is currently optimized for Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). Android and iOS versions are in development and coming later this year.
